Privacy Policy

Email Extractor Chrome Extension

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Email Extractor is a Chrome extension designed to help users extract email addresses from web pages they visit. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.
All processing and extraction of email addresses is performed locally within your browser. No data is sent to any external servers or third parties.

3. Data Usage

  • The extension operates entirely on your device.
  • No email addresses or other data are transmitted, stored, or shared outside your browser.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

4. Permissions

The permissions requested by the extension (such as access to the current tab) are solely used to enable its core functionality: extracting email addresses from the web page you are viewing. No data is collected or used for any other purpose.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use or interact with any third-party services.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date and post the new policy at this location.
